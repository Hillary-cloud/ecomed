Frequently Asked Questions

How can global partners collaborate with Ecomed?

We welcome partnerships with healthcare providers, distributors, and institutions worldwide. Please contact our international business development team via the Contact Us page to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

What quality certifications do your facilities hold?

Our manufacturing facilities are WHO-GMP certified and adhere to strict international quality standards. We regularly undergo audits to ensure compliance with global regulatory requirements.

Do you offer contract manufacturing services?

Yes, Ecomed Pharma provides comprehensive contract manufacturing services for a wide range of pharmaceutical formulations. We offer end-to-end solutions from development to packaging.

How do you ensure product safety?

Product safety is our top priority. We implement rigorous quality control measures at every stage of production, from raw material sourcing to final product testing, ensuring every product meets the highest safety standards.
